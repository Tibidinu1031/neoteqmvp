Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is neuroentrepreneurship?
A: It is an improvement to the already existing field of entrepreneurship. The difference is that it: 1. Approaches it from a neuroscience perspective in order to improve startup performance and wellbeing. 2. Targets impact, service and a sense of mission. 3. Implies a holistic approach in which sustainable business practices, social responsibility and employee/community wellbeing are prioritized.
Q: What can it address wellbeing-wise?
A: Founder's depression, decision-fatigue, stress, blindspots, mental strength etc. - they all take place in the brain after all.
Q: How about performance-wise?
A: New/improved customer relationships, team performance, retention, negotiation, leadership, startup culture, and many more. Once you understand your brain, you will be able to better understand other people's behavior as well.
Q: "How does this become a trillion dollar company?"
A: By being a company committed to lifelong-learning that constantly innovates and adapts; strategic partnerships; further expansions to wearables, parenting, sports and students; new geographies and having a global perspective.
Q: "Why is this EdTech company different than the others?"
A: Compared to giants like Masterclass, Udemy, Coursera or Skillshare, this company will be completely focused on startup education.
Q: "Reforge.com also provides startup education - how is this different compared to them?"
A: 1. This company will be focused on providing brain based startup education (Courses here). 2. It will provide customers with goal and personality tests for customized learning experiences. 3. It will additionally provide an AI tool that runs scenarios based on user input. 4. It will not only provide educational materials but will also assist startups in their growth process. 5. The courses will be available 24/7/365, more affordable and high-quality at the same time.
